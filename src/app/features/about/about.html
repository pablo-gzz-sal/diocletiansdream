About us

An exceptionally unique experience.

After touring the heart of Split's Old Town and seeing Diocletian's Palace as it is today — how about stepping back to 305 AD and experiencing the palace as it was more than 1,700 years ago?

Diocletian's Dream is Croatia's first virtual reality experience and one of the biggest attractions in Split. We combine modern technology with ancient heritage inside a UNESCO World Heritage Site — showing Diocletian's Palace in the light and splendour of its former self.

This VR specialty museum tells part of the Diocletian story in a new and original way, opening a completely new perspective on cultural and historical heritage. Through a 15-minute animated film, Severin — Diocletian's slave — takes you on a tour of the royal chambers, presenting fascinating facts about Diocletian's life and reign as one of the Roman Empire's most powerful emperors.
